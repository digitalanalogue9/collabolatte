# 3. Technology Landscape and Innovation Trends

## Emerging Technologies

Organizational network analysis is advancing toward privacy-preserving approaches (for example ego-network modeling) that can reveal structural patterns without centralizing sensitive relational data.
_Source: <https://arxiv.org/abs/2201.01290>

## Innovation Patterns

Tie structure and structural holes are linked to innovation outcomes in organizational networks, suggesting that enabling weak ties can be strategically valuable even without heavy tooling.
_Source: <https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S1751157724001482>_
